For the uninitiated,

Your First Visit

◆

Nobody is born knowing cigars, and nobody at Bloom's expects you to be. The house keeps thousands in every shape and size, which means there is a gem for every occasion and every purse, and a wall that can look like homework. It is not. Walk in, say it is your first time, and the oldest cigar shop in Pittsburgh will do what it has done since 1955: take care of its guest.

The Manner of a Visit

◆
I.Walk in and say it is your first time. No membership, no dress code, no quiz.
