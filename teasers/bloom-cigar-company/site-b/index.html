Come as you are. Leave feeling like a big shot.

Seventy years on the South Side will teach a shop how to treat a guest. A good chair, a straight answer, and the largest selection of cigars in Pittsburgh, retail and wholesale, kept fresh in a walk-in humidor that runs the length of the store. If a stick ever disappoints, it comes back for a full credit, refund, or replacement. Your call.
