The Story

Before "cigar lounge" was a term, there was Bloom's.

A 1996 profile put it plainly: the buy-a-cigar, stay-and-smoke-it shop was pioneered in part right here in the Steel City, at Bloom's, and later copied by many others. Seventy years on, the shop still runs the same way. Straight information about cigars, fair treatment, no nonsense.

1955Incorporated on the South Side

Bloom's opens at 12th Street, a block off East Carson, and never leaves the neighborhood.

The NinetiesCigar Camp™ is born

Mark's Saturday bookkeeping days turn into a standing smoke-filled tradition that outlasts the 90s cigar boom itself.

TodayThe city's biggest selection

Retail and wholesale. Bloom's moves more cigars than anyone around, which is why nothing you pick up here sits around going stale.

The Shop

Front of the shop to the back. All of it cigars.

The walk-in humidor runs the length of the store and the whole building is climate controlled. If a stick ever disappoints, it comes back for a full credit, refund, or replacement. Your call.

The Lounge

Saturdays at Bloom's

Cigar Camp™

It started as a bookkeeping day with a few friends and cigars. Decades later it is a Pittsburgh institution: the shop fills up, the smoke rolls, and the conversation takes care of itself. If you have never been, come by on a Saturday and see what the fuss is about.
