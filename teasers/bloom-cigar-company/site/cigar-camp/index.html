Saturdays at Bloom's · a Pittsburgh institution

Cigar Camp™

The shop fills up, the smoke rolls, and the conversation takes care of itself. It has been going on nearly every Saturday for over thirty years.

Come this Saturday Back to the shop
How it started

It was a bookkeeping day.

Every Saturday, a few of Mark's friends would come keep him company while he did the books: smoke a few cigars, shoot the bull. The visitors kept multiplying until a core group formed, and the bookkeeping day quietly became the main event. By the mid-90s a local paper was already calling Bloom's the pioneer of the whole sit-and-smoke shop idea, "later copied by many others."

"It was a bookkeeping day for me."

Mark, on how Cigar Camp began
How it works

No sign-up. No dues. Just show up.

Step one

Come in on a Saturday

The room does the rest. Regulars, first-timers, and whoever wandered in off Carson Street.

Step two

Pick your smoke

Grab something off the shelf or tell Mark what you like and let him hand you the right stick.

Step three

Sit a spell

Light up, join the conversation, stay as long as you want. Everyone here was new once.

Camp dates, specials, and event nights would post right here, updated by the shop in seconds.
