Attirer Grandeur

01/04 The brief Attirer Grandeur

Attirer Grandeur is a Pittsburgh music collective, and the platform behind it is ours end to end. We designed it, built it, and run it in production: artist pages, a release catalog, events, a gallery, a shop, and a membership program, all on one codebase.

It's the project where we are the client — which makes it the most honest thing in this portfolio. Every feature exists because the business needed it, and it has to keep working every day, not just on launch day.

  • StackNext.js 15 + database backend
  • PaymentsStripe — subscriptions & checkout
  • MotionGSAP, used as feedback
  • RoleDesigned, built, and operated by AP.haus

02/04 What we did Attirer Grandeur

01.

A full application, not a brochure

Built on Next.js with a database backend — artists, releases, events, gallery, and member accounts, all rendered fast. This is the difference between a website and a platform.

02.

Membership and payments on Stripe

Membership tiers run on Stripe subscriptions; the shop runs on Stripe checkout. Recurring billing, one-off payments, and the account plumbing that connects them — already in our toolbox for any client who needs to take money online.

03.

Designed like a label, not a template

An editorial identity — oversized serif initials, monospace detailing, a restrained cream-and-red palette — with GSAP motion used as feedback, not decoration.

04.

Operated, not handed off

We run this platform daily: content, releases, payments, upkeep. When we tell a client we'll maintain their site under a care plan, this is the standing evidence.
