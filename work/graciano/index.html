Graciano

01/04 The brief Graciano

Graciano Corporation is one of the oldest masonry restoration firms in the country — landmark work across Pittsburgh, New York, and Sarasota since 1916. Its public site runs on aging WordPress and undersells a century of craft.

We rebuilt it as a concept: a demonstration of what our free homepage mockup produces when you take it all the way. This is a working demo, built as a showcase — Graciano is the subject of the rebuild, not a client.

  • StackStatic HTML/CSS, no CMS
  • SEO pages30+ service & location pages, script-generated
  • TurnaroundOne week
  • HostingGitHub Pages (demo)

02/04 What we did Graciano

01.

An editorial system worthy of the subject

Serif display type and a deep green and stone palette drawn from the firm's identity, with a structure that leads with heritage and the project portfolio instead of a plugin-built template.

02.

Static pages, no WordPress

The demo is plain, fast, static HTML — no CMS, no plugin stack, nothing to patch or break. It loads like a page from a company that has its act together.

03.

A local-SEO page system

A script-generated set of 30+ service and location pages — tuckpointing, terra cotta, facade inspection, Local Law 11 — each a real, complete page targeting a real search, not doorway filler.

04.

The before/after you can drag

The rebuild sits beside the original on our homepage's draggable slider — the fastest honest way to show what a rebuild actually changes.
