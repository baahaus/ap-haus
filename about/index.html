AP.haus is Brandon and Cam — two friends from Pittsburgh who have been building things together since first grade.

These days that means websites, booking systems, and the automation that keeps a small business's office running. Brandon handles design, copy, and the client side; Cam handles engineering. Between the two of us your project gets a designer, a developer, a writer, and a systems person — without an agency invoice or an account manager in the middle.

We keep the studio deliberately small. A few projects at a time, built start to finish by the people you actually talk to. When your site ships, most clients keep us on a small monthly care plan and simply text when they need something changed.

The engineering runs deeper than local business sites — we build and run our own commerce and membership platforms in production, and some of our tools live in the Lab. Your project gets all of that, at local-business prices.
